Skiing

Why do I love skiing?

Well in truth I didn't always like to ski. My parents made me participate in skiing classes at a very young age. Of course I wasn't a huge fan of being forced into a sport, even though it did give me quite a headstart for in my older years. Once I reached the age of about 10 to 12 I actually started to enjoy skiing. Afterwards, when I was able to join the older groups and actively do more enjoyable skiing activities I started to really love it, going as far to even become a skiing instructor once I became 17.

My favourite parts of skiing lately have become more relaxed. I used to be really into slalom and technical skiing. I never truly got into freestyle skiing and doing tricks.
